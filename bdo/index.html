— The Role

Why the BDO
Matters

Mission

Bridge the Gap

Actively onboard underserved communities into trusted digital and banking platforms — bringing financial access to small businesses, individuals, and remote areas.

Vision

Nationwide Champion

To be a nationwide champion of financial inclusion — where every Filipino, regardless of location or status, has access to secure, reliable, and innovative financial services.

Impact

Real-World Change

BDOs open doors to opportunity, educate merchants, and expand the reach of inclusive finance — ensuring iPROCESS reaches the grassroots, one merchant at a time.

— Core Values

The iProcess Way


Six values every BDO lives by — not just principles on paper, but daily practice in the field.

01

Integrity

Honesty and transparency in every engagement — we do the right thing, even when no one is watching.

02

Performance Excellence

Meeting and exceeding daily targets with consistency, discipline, and accountability — success measured by impact.

03

Financial Inclusion

Every Filipino deserves access to financial services. We champion inclusivity by bringing digital finance to underserved communities.

04

Customer Empowerment

Every merchant and client is treated with respect — guided, informed, and confident in using our services.

05

Growth & Innovation

Continuous learning, adaptability, and improvement — staying ahead in a rapidly evolving financial technology landscape.

06

Teamwork & Leadership

We lead with purpose and work as one. Collaboration with supervisors, colleagues, and clients makes our mission stronger.
