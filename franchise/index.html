— Requirements

iProcess Business Center Franchise Requirements

Three pillars every applicant must satisfy before becoming an iProcess franchise partner.

Location Requirements

  • – High foot traffic — commercial areas, shopping centers, transportation hubs
  • – Accessible by public transport with ample parking
  • – Commercial space with room for queues, waiting areas, and counters
  • – Prominent visibility for brand exposure
  • – Safe neighborhood for customers and staff

* Specific requirements may vary. Consult our franchise representatives.

Population Requirements

  • – Minimum population density ensuring a steady stream of customers
  • – Diverse demographics with wide-ranging financial service needs

* Specific requirements may vary. Consult our franchise representatives.

Applicant Requirements

  • – Entrepreneurial drive and commitment to growing a successful business
  • – Financial capability to cover franchise fees, setup, and operational costs
  • – Sound understanding of business operations and management
  • – Familiarity with the local market and community
  • – Ethical conduct — transparency, integrity, and commitment to service

* Specific requirements may vary. Consult our franchise representatives.
