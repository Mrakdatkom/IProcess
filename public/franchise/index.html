— Requirements

iProcess Business Center Franchise Requirements

Location Requirements

  • • High Foot Traffic – commercial areas, shopping centers, transportation hubs
  • • Accessibility – public transport & ample parking
  • • Commercial Space – room for queues, waiting areas, counters
  • • Visibility – prominent location for brand exposure
  • • Security – safe neighborhood for customers and staff

* Specific requirements may vary. Consult with our franchise representatives.

Population Requirements

  • • Minimum Population Density – steady stream of customers
  • • Diverse Demographics – wide range of financial needs

* Specific requirements may vary. Consult with our franchise representatives.

Applicant Requirements

  • • Entrepreneurial Drive – commitment to growing a successful business
  • • Financial Capability – cover franchise fees, setup, operational costs
  • • Business Knowledge – understanding of business operations
  • • Local Knowledge – familiarity with the local market
  • • Ethical Conduct – uphold transparency, integrity, and service

* Specific requirements may vary. Consult with our franchise representatives.
